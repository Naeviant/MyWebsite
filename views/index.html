About Me


Right from an early age, I have had an interest in IT and have been working to build on my skills independently. Whether it be using Microsoft Office creatively, or building a website such as this one, most of my knowledge comes from what I have taught myself.

Having recently achieved A*A*A*A at A-Level in Computer Science, Maths, EPQ and Physics, I am now beginning a degree in Computer Science. I am now looking to begin some part-time work alongside this degree in order to further improve my skills and build my experience.

Most of my independent work is centered around Computer Science. My speciality is web development - specifically using the MERN stack. In the past I have also created desktop applications in VB.net and C#, as well as basic programs using Batch and Python.

This site is designed to act as an online portfolio for my work. Choosing the Projects button on the homepage will show you some of my recent work. More are available if you're interested, so please get in touch if you would like to see the rest.
